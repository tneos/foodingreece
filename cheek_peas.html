Chickpea soup

VERY EASY

PREP: 10MINS
COOK: 1 HOUR

SERVES 4

One of the most popular dishes, especially in winter, chickpea soup is a highly nutritious option, full of fibre, minerals and protein. Very easy to make, although it takes a lot longer cooking time. Suitable for vegans and in high demand during the fasting period in Greece one week before Easter.

Ingredients

500g chickpeas
1 large onion finely chopped
3 bay leaves
juice of 1 lemon
100ml extra virgin olive oil
a sprinkle of oregano
2 bay leaves
1tsp flour
salt, pepper
1tsp bicarbonate of soda

Method

1. Soak the chickpeas the night before in a large bowl with water and a teaspoon of salt and leave them over night.
2. Drain the chickpeas the day after and rinse them well .Place them in a large pan with 1.5 lt of water.
3.Add the onion, bay leaves, oregano,and olive oil, season the soup and bring it to the boil in medium heat for about 45 min.
4. Add the lemon juice in a large pot along with the flour and stir to combine.
5. Add the lemon mix and also 1 teaspoon of baking soda and cook in low heat for 15-20 more min.
