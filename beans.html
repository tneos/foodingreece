Beans

EASY

PREP: 10MINS
COOK: 25MINS

SERVES 4

Beans are quite common in Greek cuisine. There is a number of recipes using different varieties of beans and the most well known one is 'Fasolada' (bean soup). Easy to make, a perfect option on a cold winter day. In this recipe we are using kidney beans instead, in a similar way.

Ingredients

500g kidney or butter beans
1 large onion finely chopped
2 garlic cloves minced
2 medium carrots chopped
100ml extra virgin olive oil
1/2 red pepper sliced
30g flat leaf parsley finely chopped
4 tbsp tomato pasta sauce
a sprinkle of oregano
salt, pepper
a pinch of chilli flakes(optional)

Method

1. Place the beans in a large pan with with cold water that covers half of it and leave them overnight to soak.Drain in colander the day after and cover them in hot water(1.5lt)
2. Finely chop the onions,parsley and carrots, slice the pepper and add them all along with olive oil and bring it up to a boil on a medium heat for about 30 min.
3. Add tomato sauce 15 min later, season and cook the soup until the beans are tender.
4. Just before the end if you feel your mix is very thick, add another glass of water and let it simmer for a few more min.
5. Serve in bowls while hot, perfect combination with feta or olives.
