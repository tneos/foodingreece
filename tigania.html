Fried pork chops and handmade chips

VERY EASY

PREP: 15MINS
COOK: 25MINS

SERVES 2

A traditional Greek recipe served as main meal along with chips or rice ,and also as an appetizer especially on Christmas day, it's a simple way of frying pork chops with herbs and lemon which gives the extra flavour to the meat.

Ingredients

500g pork chops cut into chunks
1 large onion cut into slices
1tsp dried thyme
1tsp oregano
2tbsp extra virgin olive oil (for the chops) , 100ml extra virgin olive oil (for the chips)
1 lemon zested and juiced
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into thin chip
salt, pepper

Method

1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large frying pan, add the onion, then gently cook for about 5 mins until it gets soft. Meanwhile, sprinkle the meat on all sides with salt, pepper and oregano.
2. Add the pork chops and sear the meat on both sides until nicely browned. .
3. Add the lemon juice in a large pot along with a glass of water and stir to combine. Add the mix to the pan and slow cook for 20 min.
4. Heat the olive oil on medium heat , add the potatoes and sprikle with salt. Stir gently a few times so that the potatoes do not stick. Keep the heat to medium-low and fry the potatoes for about 15 min.
