Spinach and eggs

VERY EASY

PREP: 10MINS
COOK: 15MINS

SERVES 2

A very simple recipe which is not well known everywhere in Greece, but it's one of the notable dishes around Pelion peninsula, where there's a large variety of fresh green vegetables to use for this delicious meal.


Ingredients

250g young or whole leaf spinach
100g spring onions
4tbsp chopped parsley
4tbsp chopped dill
2tbsp chopped fennel
4 medium eggs
3tbsp tomato sauce
extra virgin olive oil
salt, pepper

Method

1. Finely chop all the veg.
2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add in onions and cook until transparent.
3. Add all the green vegetables, stir for a couple of minutes, and then add tomato sauce and a glass of water. Season with salt and black pepper. Cook in medium to low heat stirring gently for 10 min until sauce is thickened and veg tender.
4. Add the eggs on top of the mix ,sprinkle salt and pepper, and cook for 5 more minutes until the eggs are quite hard.
5. Serve hot.
