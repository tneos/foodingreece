Lentil soup

EASY

PREP: 10MINS
COOK: 25MINS

SERVES 4

One of the most traditional soups, lentils are a great source of energy and suitable for vegetarians or vegans. A very low cost recipe which is a great source of fibre and proteins.

Ingredients

200g kidney or butter beans
1 large onion
3 garlic cloves
2 carrots
1 potato
100ml extra virgin olive oil
a sprinkle of oregano
2 bay leaves
4tbsp tomato sauce
salt, 1tsp vinegar

Method

1. Peel and finely chop the onion and the carrots. Peel and slice the garlic.
2. Place lentils in a large saucepan, add about 1.5lt water and bring it to a boil along with the veg, olive oil and oregano.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low after 15 min and add tomato sauce and the bay leaves. Still well and season.
4. Just before the end if you feel your mix is very thick, add another glass of water and let it simmer for a few more min.
5. Serve in bowls while hot, perfect combination with feta or olives.
